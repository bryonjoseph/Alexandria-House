Alexandria House

Alexandria House is a transitional residence for single women and women with children in the process of moving from emergency shelter to permanent housing. We provide hospitality to approximately 24 women and children depending on family size. Women living in Alexandria House benefit from a supportive community and an encouraging environment.
Residents meet weekly with a Resident Services Coordinator (Case Manager) to ensure that they are making progress towards their established goals, as well as to identify their need for services and resources.
Weekly house meetings also provide an opportunity to share personal successes and hopes, and at the same time build community.
Alexandria House's transitional housing program offers financial literacy, goal setting, childcare, counseling, healthcare, and access to other resources such as employment assistance, education, legal counsel, advocacy, and skill-building.
Over 80% of the women who have moved through the program have succeeded in securing financial stability and permanent housing.

In response to the compelling needs of the women and children living in the house, as well as the neighborhood needs before us each day, the goal of Alexandria House is to be a community-oriented transitional house and neighborhood center which is intentionally multicultural, antiracist and where the needs of women who are economically poor are central.


Services include: - Permanent Housing Assistance - Intensive Case Management - Employment Assistance - Financial Literacy - Food Pantry - On-site Child Care - After School Tutoring - Adult & Children's Support Groups - Community Information & Referral - Community Workshops - "Soul Sistas Network" - Past Residents Alumni Group - Past Residents Support Services - Community Thrift Shop (2nd Saturday of each month)

Volunteer Opportunities

Partners In Service: WE NEED YOU!!

Volunteer Orientations will be held every 1st Tuesday of the month at 7:00pm and every 3rd Sunday of the month at 10:30am. Please RSVP to Yajaira@Alexandriahouse.org PLEASE NOTE: No orientation on Sunday, April 17, 2016 Our volunteer opportunities are listed below, however these are currently our top priority: We need volunteers for Thursday nights from 7:30pm - 10:00pm for about 12 kids ranging in ages 1 to 13 years old. Must be ready to assume control and organize their free time. We would like 3 volunteers. We need volunteers for Childcare Monday - Thursday 9:00am - 2:00pm. We are looking for volunteers who are capable of working with toddlers and kids up to age 4. We need volunteers for the Afterschool program Monday - Friday 3:30pm - 5:30pm. Volunteers should be at least high school age but we may consider middle schoolers. We need Tutors for the Teen Program Monday - Friday 5:30pm - 8:00pm. Volunteers should be willing to meet with student/s they are tutoring once/twice a week for one hour. Thank you for your consideration and support!

Top Chef – The number one requested volunteer service is cooking for a night. It is a wonderful treat for women living in the house when volunteers come to cook dinner. Just like you, they have busy days with work, school and kids and can use a “night off” every once in a while. Bring food in, cook in our kitchen...make it work for you knowing that it will definitely work for us! Thrift Sale – Please don't forget our neighborhood thrift sale when cleaning out your closets or updating your décor. We will gladly take your gently used items and turn them into profit for the house. Don't forget to shop there, too! There are great bargains to be had! (Every 2nd Saturday of the month, 9am – Noon). After-School & Teen Programs – We always need volunteers to help with our programs for children and youth. You don't need special skills or equipment, just bring your love of children, learning and play. Drivers Needed! – Many times our residents need rides to appointments or job interviews and sometimes our afterschool kids need rides to field trips. Can you help? If you're a licensed, insured driver, we need you! Target Gift Cards – We create a home-like environment by celebrating the important milestones in residents lives: birthday, graduations, special days. Rather than shop for gifts, we have found that residents feel more empowered by selecting what they need for themselves or for their children. Target gift cards in $20 amounts are our gift of choice and can always use them. Host a Fund Raiser! – We are always looking for new ways to increase our supporters. We have the ideas and ability—you provide the friends! What’s Your Talent? – Maybe you have a special talent or ability and can share your gift with us! Let us know if you have a talent you would like to share!
